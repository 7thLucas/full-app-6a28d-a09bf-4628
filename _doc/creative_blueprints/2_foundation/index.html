The Case for CoachIQ

Member churn starts with inconsistent coaches.

The Problem

Franchise owners can't see coach quality across studios.

Running multiple boutique fitness studios means you never know if your coaches are actually delivering the brand experience — until a member cancels. Quality problems go undetected until they compound into churn, negative reviews, and declining retention across every location.

No visibility into coach delivery quality at scale
Missed service steps and scripts go unnoticed for weeks
Front-desk inconsistency silently drives member churn
Coaching feels subjective — no data behind feedback
The Solution

AI reviews every session. You see every gap.
